# RentFlexNG

## Housing Access Infrastructure for Africa

RentFlexNG is a PropTech platform that helps prospective tenants plan, fund, verify, secure, and acquire rental accommodation without the burden of raising a full year's rent upfront.

The platform enables users to build their rent fund through structured payments, undergo verification, access verified properties, and move into their desired homes once their rent obligation has been completed.

RentFlexNG serves as a trusted ecosystem connecting Tenants, Property Owners, Real Estate Companies, Agents, Employers, and Administrators through a transparent digital workflow.

---

# Vision

To become Africa's most trusted housing access and property infrastructure platform.

---

# Mission

To simplify access to quality housing through technology-driven rent planning, verification, property management, and digital real estate services.

---

# Problem Statement

In many African countries, tenants are required to pay one or more years of rent upfront despite earning monthly incomes.

This creates:

* Financial strain
* Dependence on loans
* Housing insecurity
* Delayed relocation opportunities
* Reduced access to quality housing

Property owners also face challenges such as:

* Tenant verification difficulties
* Property vacancy periods
* Manual documentation processes
* Limited visibility into tenant quality

RentFlexNG addresses these challenges through structured rent planning, verification systems, and digital property services.

---

# Core Solution

RentFlexNG allows qualified users to:

1. Register and complete verification.
2. Create a rent acquisition plan.
3. Make an initial commitment contribution.
4. Complete their target rent through structured payments.
5. Access verified property opportunities.
6. Complete tenancy documentation.
7. Move into their selected property.

# Platform Workflow

## Step 1 - Registration

The user creates an account and selects their role.

Supported roles include:

* Tenant
* Property Owner
* Agent
* Employer
* Administrator

---

## Step 2 - Verification

The user completes identity and affordability verification.

Verification may include:

* National Identification Number (NIN)
* Bank Verification Number (BVN)
* Government-issued Identification
* Employment Verification
* Income Assessment
* Contact Verification

---

## Step 3 - Rent Plan Activation

The tenant creates a rent goal by providing:

* Desired location
* Apartment type
* Target move-in date
* Target annual rent amount

The system calculates:

* Commitment amount
* Remaining balance
* Installment schedule
* Completion timeline

---

## Step 4 - Structured Payments

The tenant completes their rent plan through scheduled payments.

Features include:

* Progress Tracking
* Payment Reminders
* Installment Monitoring
* Account Statements

---

## Step 5 - Property Matching

Eligible users gain access to:

* Verified Properties
* Property Inspections
* Reservation Requests
* Property Recommendations

---

## Step 6 - Tenancy Completion

Upon satisfying payment requirements:

* Agreements are generated
* Documentation is completed
* Property handover is initiated
